How to play

Simon is a classic electronic game that tests your memory and attention. The game consists of a circular device with four colored buttons: red, green, blue and yellow. Each button makes a distinct sound when pressed.

  • Press the play () button to start the game.
  • The computer () will play a sequence of notes. The first turn will just be one note.
    • Be sure to pay attention to what the computer plays!
  • Click on the game pieces in the same order that the computer played.
  • If you got all of the pieces in the correct order the computer will play the same sequence again adding one more piece.
  • The score you receive is based on the number of buttons in the sequence that the player successfully repeats.
    • For example, if the device plays red-green-blue and the player presses red-green-blue, they score 3 points.
  • You lose the game when you make a mistake.
